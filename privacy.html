Privacy Policy

Effective July 22, 2026 · Package com.skroll.seeker

Skroll (“Skroll”, “we”, “us”) is a short video application for Solana Seeker. This policy explains what information the app processes and how it is used.

1. Controllers

Skroll processes limited technical and on chain data needed to run the service. Wallet keys stay in your Seeker Seed Vault or connected wallet. Skroll does not receive or store seed phrases or private keys.

2. Data we process

  • Wallet public key when you connect Seeker Wallet to tip, publish, report, or manage clips.
  • Video and captions you upload, stored on Cloudflare R2 via the Skroll upload worker.
  • Device media permissions camera, microphone, and gallery, used only for recording or importing clips.
  • Reports reason codes and reporter wallet for moderation.
  • On chain tip data amounts, memo tags, and related Solana transaction metadata visible on the public ledger.
  • Local preferences such as language and locally hidden reported clips on your device.

3. What we do not collect

  • No seed phrases or private keys.
  • No government ID or KYC documents.
  • No advertising ID sales to third party ad networks.
  • No contacts list scraping.

4. Why we process data

  • Publish and stream clips on the Skroll feed.
  • Enable SKR tips, retention extensions, and featured boosts.
  • Moderate abuse via reports and auto hide rules.
  • Operate storage retention (default TTL and paid extensions).
  • Secure the service and prevent fraud or spam.

5. Storage and retention

Media objects live in Cloudflare R2 through the Skroll worker. Clips expire after the configured retention window unless extended. Report records are kept to enforce moderation. On chain transfers remain public forever on Solana.

6. Sharing

We use infrastructure providers (for example Cloudflare) to host media and the upload API. Public feed URLs and Solana transactions are visible to anyone. We do not sell personal data.

7. Your choices

  • Disconnect your wallet in Settings.
  • Delete your own clips from Profile when available.
  • Revoke camera, microphone, or gallery access in Android settings.
  • Report abusive content from the feed.

8. Children

Skroll is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). Do not use the app if you are under that age.

9. Changes

We may update this policy as the product evolves. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy. Material changes will be noted by updating the effective date.

10. Contact

For privacy requests related to Skroll, open an issue on the project repository github.com/DuckerForge/skroll or contact the publisher wallet listed in the Seeker Store listing.
